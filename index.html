A Call to the Field

With great honour we announce the founding of the United States Polo Patrons Association. This new fellowship of patrons and players begins with the launch of Polo Patronium, a living token of support and tradition. Together we set forth to celebrate the sport of polo, foster its growth, and open a new chapter in the storied life of the game.

Making history as the first polo club and association founded with blockchain technology for transparency and decentralization.
