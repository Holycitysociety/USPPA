Patronium Model

Stewardship-Based Rewards

Patronium is a utility token and seal of participation ‚Äî not a security or speculative instrument. It rewards those who walk with us through principled alignment, not entitlement. Rewards may be distributed manually from active Polo Chapters when the mission allows.

Tribute Allocation Framework

Each active Chapter follows a 51/49 principle:
51% ‚Äî Reinvested into horses, pasture, and operations
24.5% ‚Äî Steward‚Äôs draw or groom-equivalent wage
24.5% ‚Äî USPPA tribute pool for Patronium holders

In the first year, 49% flows to Founding Patrons instead ‚Äî rewarding those who helped launch.

Claim, Don‚Äôt Stake

Rewards are earned, not promised. There is no staking, no yield farming, and no expectation of income. Snapshots may be taken to determine eligibility, and rewards are manually claimable based on alignment and stewardship.

Chapter Pipeline

USPPA Chapters begin with a Steward ‚Äî a trained or approved individual ‚Äî and a Polo Incubator funded by Founding Patrons. After 12 months of operations and public play, rewards stabilize, and the local operation matures into a Chapter.

Local stewards may be compensated with a groom-level wage or retain up to 24.5% of the net revenue, whichever is greater.

Polo Patronage Perfected

The United States Polo Patrons Association (USPPA) has been established to restore American polo as a noble, public-facing pursuit ‚Äî one not confined to the elite, but grounded in community, stewardship, and sport.

I. What Is a USPPA Chapter?

A USPPA Chapter is a locally operated, nationally governed polo programme seeded through a Polo Incubator. Each chapter is:

  • Wholly owned by USPPA ‚Äî no franchising or outside equity
  • Operated by a trained or approved steward
  • Funded by Founding Patrons through tribute
  • Vertically integrated and mission-aligned

III. Patronium Explained

Patronium is not a token of entitlement. It does not promise income, governance rights, or ownership. It is a seal of alignment ‚Äî recognition of contribution to the game‚Äôs rebirth.

  • No staking required
  • No automatic yield
  • No profit-sharing or governance rights

IV. How Rewards Work

  • When rewards are loaded, a snapshot of Patronium holders is taken
  • Founding Patron wallets may be boosted via whitelist
  • Manual claim button must be clicked by the user
  • Anti-abuse mechanisms prevent reward gaming via token transfer
