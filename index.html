A Call to Take the Field

With great honor we announce the founding of the United States Polo Patrons Association. This new fellowship of patrons and players begins with the launch of Polo Patronium, a living token of support and tradition. Together we set forth to celebrate the sport of polo, foster its growth, and open a new chapter in the storied life of the game.


Polo Patronium

The Patronium token and membership NFTs — uniting patrons, players, and teams in a shared economy of sport.

The Polo Life

A dedicated streaming and media platform, bringing polo’s elegance and tradition to the digital age.

Charleston Polo

The founding of our flagship in Charleston, South Carolina — restoring the city’s polo tradition with renewed vigour.

Cowboy Polo Circuit

A bold initiative to lower the barrier of entry, expose new talent across America’s heartland, and grow the next generation of U.S. ten-goal players.
