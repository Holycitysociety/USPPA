A Call to the Field

With great honour we announce the founding of the United States Polo Patrons Association. This new fellowship of patrons and players begins with the launch of Polo Patronium, a living token of support and tradition. Together we set forth to celebrate the sport of polo, foster its growth, and open a new chapter in the storied life of the game.

Polo Patronium

The Patronium token and Membership NFTs — uniting patrons, players, and clubs in a shared economy of sport.

The Polo Life

Our streaming platform dedicated to telling the stories of polo, its patrons, and players — celebrating the culture of the sport and bringing it to new audiences.

Charleston Polo Club

The founding club of the Association, re-establishing Charleston’s historic polo tradition and anchoring the national fellowship of patrons.

“Polo is more than a game — it is heritage, fellowship, and the charge of tradition carried into the future.”
